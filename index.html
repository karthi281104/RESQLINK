About RESQLINK

RESQLINK was developed to address the critical communication challenges faced during disaster situations, trekking expeditions, and remote area operations where traditional networks become unavailable.

Our hardware solution utilizes LoRa frequency technology to transmit vital data like location coordinates, status updates, and emergency messages without requiring cellular or internet connectivity.

By bridging the communication gap during emergencies, we enable faster response times, better coordination between rescue teams, and ultimately save more lives in critical situations.
